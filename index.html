Planet Descriptions

Sun: The center of our solar system.

Mercury: The smallest planet, closest to the sun.

Venus: The hottest planet, covered in thick clouds.

Earth: Our home, the only planet known to support life.

Mars: Known as the Red Planet, it may have had water in the past.

Jupiter: The largest planet with a famous Great Red Spot.

Saturn: Known for its stunning rings made of ice and rock.

Uranus: A tilted planet, rotates on its side.

Neptune: The coldest planet, with strong winds and storms.
